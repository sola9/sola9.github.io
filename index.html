About

I'm interested in a wide variety of things - nature, writing, gaming, making personal webpages with oversized headers... My username here, sola9, refers to solanine, a toxin produced by nightshades, which are some of my favorite plants. Unfortunately I came up with the name thinking that the "nine" in solanine was pronounced the same way as the number 9, but it isn't, which leaves me in the rather unfortunate position of not knowing how to pronounce my own name.
